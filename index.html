Quick Rules:

In Wordle, players are tasked with deciphering a secret five-letter word within a limited number of attempts. Each guess is met with specific feedback: correct letters that are also in the correct position are highlighted, while correct letters in the wrong position are marked differently. Incorrect letters are indicated as well. Players strategically select words based on these cues, gradually narrowing down the possibilities with each guess. The challenge lies in deciphering the word using logic and deduction within the allotted attempts.
